Criteria for Admission :

    For Class Playgroup

  1. Submission of Registration form

  2. Testimonials required:
    • Copy of Birth certificate
    • Copy of Residence proof
    • Copy of Medical Fitness certificate
    • Three Passphoto of Child

  3. Interaction of child and parents with principal and chairperson on the assigned date.

  4. Parents are informed about the status of admission.

  5. After admission is granted, fees to be deposited within allotted time.

    For class I-V

  1. Submission of Registration form
  2. Child has to appear for an Informal Dkill_Knowledge index round
  3. Parents to be informed about the status of admission within 2 days.
  4. Interaction date with Pincipal or Principal maam(date to be given to parents on phone)
  5. After admission is granted , fees to be deposited within allotted time
  6. Testimonials required:
    • Copy of birth certificate
    • Copy of Medical Fitness certificate
    • Three passphoto of child
    • Transfer certificate in original
