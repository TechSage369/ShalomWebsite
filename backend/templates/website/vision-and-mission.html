Our Vision and Mission

Shalom English School is an English medium co-education school based on Christian faith which was started in year 2010 in the grace of Almighty God with a vision to provide the holistic education to meet the inevitable demand of this competitive century. Although the school is recognized as a Christian School it opens its door for all the children irrespective of Religion, Caste, or Community.

The School bears the Motto:

Honesty, Excellence, and Peace

The school will be striving continuously towards making its vision and mission true by equipping each child with the advanced method teaching and technology. Hence, some of the features of the school to make its vision and mission come true are:

1.Offers comprehensive educition in play way method.

2.Develops each child's skill in loving environment.

3.Pleasant and activity based classes where learning is fun.

4.Manageable clean and hygienic classrooms.

5.Healthy envirinment of acceptance and integrity to help the children to promote the feelings of self-respect, sharing and caring among the students.

6.Individual care and attention to each child will be given.

7.Offers different types of co-curricular activities for e.g.,Arts and craft, Music and Dance, Games and Sports etc.

8.Audio-Visual classes to promote the language skill of the children.

9.English is spoken compulsorily as a medium of communication in the school premises.
